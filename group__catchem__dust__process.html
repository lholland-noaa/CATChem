This is the FENGSHA dust emission scheme developed at NOAA Air Resources Laboratory.

This is the FENGSHA dust emission scheme developed at NOAA Air Resources Laboratory. Originally developed by Daniel Tong and revised by Barry Baker. FENGSHA is implemented operationally at the NOAA National Weather Service in the Global Ensamble Forecast System (GEFS) version 12 and has been implemented in some fashion in the National Air Quality Forecast System Capability (NAQFC) since 2012.

outputs emission flux in [ug m-2 s-1]
